Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions about custom songs, timelines, revisions, and what to expect.

Questions

How long does it take to create a song?

Most songs take about 2–4 weeks from the time you share your story, depending on complexity and schedule. Rush options may be available if your date is close.

What do you need from me?

You do not need to know anything about music. I’ll send you a few simple questions about your story, your partner, and the moments that matter. Honest details are what make the song powerful.

Can I request changes?

Yes. Each song includes a round of reasonable revisions for lyrics and minor musical tweaks. The goal is for it to feel like you.

Do I own the song?

You receive a license to use the song for personal events, social media, and sharing with friends and family. For commercial or wider use, we can discuss options.

Can you match a certain style or artist?

I can lean into a vibe or general style you like, while still keeping the song original and personal to you.

What if I’m not sure what I want yet?

That’s fine. Tell me your date, your story, and what you’re imagining. I can suggest the best tier and approach for what you’re trying to create.
